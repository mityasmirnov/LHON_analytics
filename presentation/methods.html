Mathematical Modeling Framework

Three complementary modeling approaches:

  • Liability-Threshold Model
    • Integrates genetic and environmental risk factors
    • Disease occurs when liability exceeds threshold
  • Bayesian Hierarchical Network
    • Probabilistic relationships between factors
    • Accounts for parameter uncertainty
  • Monte Carlo Population Simulation
    • Agent-based modeling of 100,000 individuals
    • Bridges individual risk with population epidemiology
