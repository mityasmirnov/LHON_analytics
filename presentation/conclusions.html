Conclusions

Key findings:

  • LHON penetrance is dramatically lower than traditionally estimated: ~2.44% vs. 40-50%
  • Over 98% of pathogenic variant carriers remain asymptomatic
  • Individual risk varies by >1000-fold based on genetic and environmental factors
  • Modifiable risk factors (smoking, alcohol) significantly impact risk

Paradigm Shift:

LHON should be reconceptualized as a low-penetrance, multifactorial disease rather than a high-risk Mendelian condition

Clinical implications:

Genetic counseling should shift toward personalized, probabilistic risk assessment that emphasizes modifiable risk factors
