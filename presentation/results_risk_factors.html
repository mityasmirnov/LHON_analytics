Risk Factor Analysis

Key risk factors affecting LHON penetrance:

  • Male sex: Strongest single predictor (OR 7.11)
  • Environmental factors:
    • Heavy smoking (>20 cig/day): OR 3.16
    • Heavy alcohol (>35 units/week): OR 3.25
    • Combined heavy smoking + alcohol: OR 8.95
  • Genetic modifiers:
    • Haplogroup J: OR 2.00 (for m.11778G>A, m.14484T>C)
    • Heteroplasmy: Protective (OR 0.37)

Clinical implications:

Risk varies by >1000-fold based on individual risk profiles, necessitating personalized risk assessment
