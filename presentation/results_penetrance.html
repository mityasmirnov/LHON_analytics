Revised Penetrance Estimates

Key findings:

  • Overall LHON penetrance: ~2.44% (95% CI: 1.8-3.1%)
  • Dramatic reduction from traditional estimates (40-50%)
  • Variant-specific penetrance:
    • m.3460G>A: 17.15% (vs. 78% in literature)
    • m.11778G>A: 4.56% (vs. 43% in literature)
    • m.14484T>C: 0.61% (vs. 65% in literature)
  • Sex-specific differences:
    • Males: 4.5% overall penetrance
    • Females: 0.5% overall penetrance
